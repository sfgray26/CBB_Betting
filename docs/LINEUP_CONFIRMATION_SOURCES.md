# Daily MLB Lineup Confirmation Sources (2026 Season)

## 1. Overview
The most critical timeframe for Daily Fantasy Baseball and daily roster management is the 7:00 AM ET window. Reliable lineup sources during this period are essential for identifying early game stacks and late-breaking injuries.

## 2. Industry Standard Sources (7:00 AM ET Window)
| Source | URL | Reliability (7 AM ET) | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Baseball Press** | [baseballpress.com/lineups](http://www.baseballpress.com/lineups) | **High** | Often the first to post projected vs. confirmed lineups. |
| **RotoWire** | [rotowire.com/baseball/daily-lineups.php](https://www.rotowire.com/baseball/daily-lineups.php) | **High** | Integrated with salary data; provides "Confirmed" vs. "Projected" tags. |
| **MLB.com** | [mlb.com/lineups](https://www.mlb.com/lineups) | **Medium** | Official, but can lag behind dedicated fantasy sources for early projections. |

## 3. Recommended Workflow
*   **7:00 AM ET:** Check **Baseball Press** for early-bird game projections.
*   **9:00 AM ET:** Monitor **RotoWire** for "Confirmed" statuses (Green Checkmarks).
*   **12:00 PM ET:** Use **RosterResource (FanGraphs)** for deep-bench depth chart changes.

## 4. Key Automation Target
*   **Baseball Press API/Scraper:** Focus on parsing the "Projected" field early in the morning to feed the daily roster optimizer.
